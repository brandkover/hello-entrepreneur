Hello Entrepreneur inspires and motivates entrepreneurs with quotes and business tips directly on your WordPress dashboard.  

== Description ==  
Hello Entrepreneur is a plugin inspired by the classic Hello Dolly, specially designed for entrepreneurs.  
Every time you access your WordPress dashboard, you'll see motivational quotes from business leaders and practical tips to guide your entrepreneurial journey.  

**Key Features:**  
* Inspirational quotes from renowned business leaders.  
* Practical and actionable advice for entrepreneurs and freelancers.  
* Lightweight, fast, and compatible with all recent versions of WordPress.  

Perfect for those seeking daily inspiration while working on their web projects.  

== Installation ==  
1. Upload the plugin's .zip file to your WordPress site (via `Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin`).  
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu.  
3. That’s it! You’ll now see entrepreneurial inspiration on your WordPress dashboard.  

== Frequently Asked Questions ==  

= Can I customize the quotes or tips? =  
Currently, customization is not available, but we plan to include this feature in future updates.  

= Does the plugin consume a lot of resources? =  
No, Hello Entrepreneur is designed to be lightweight and efficient.
